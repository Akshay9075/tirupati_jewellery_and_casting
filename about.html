Company Introduction

A Legacy of Fine Craftsmanship

Founded in October 2023, Tirupati Jewellery & Casting has quickly established itself as a trusted name in Nashik's jewellery industry. Located in the heart of Saraf Bazar, we specialize in manufacturing and selling premium gold jewellery, elegant silver collections, and bespoke custom designs.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. From intricate traditional designs to contemporary masterpieces, we bring your jewellery visions to life with precision and artistry.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional jewellery that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation, ensuring every customer receives pieces of unmatched quality, beauty, and value.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted jewellery manufacturer and seller in Maharashtra, recognized for our craftsmanship, integrity, and dedication to creating timeless pieces that celebrate life's precious moments.
